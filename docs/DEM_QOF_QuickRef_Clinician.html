Strouts Place Medical Centre  ·  Dementia Register — Annual Care Plan Review

Version 1.0 Effective: April 2025 Review: March 2026
📋 QOF Indicator & Target
Code Indicator Pts Target Window
DEM004 Care plan reviewed in preceding 12 months 14 35–70% 12 months
💡
Use the (RP) LTC Review Tower Hamlets CEG v1.4 template in EMIS. Code the review on the day. Where no care plan exists, create one at this appointment. First review after new diagnosis should be within 6 months.
🗂️ Four Mandatory Review Domains
🫁 Physical & Mental Health
  • Physical health assessment — consider behavioural change as symptom of pain or infection
  • Screen for new dementia symptoms: wandering, delusions, hallucinations
  • Screen for depression and anxiety
  • Social care needs and referrals
  • Review cognitive stimulation therapy (CST) — offered / ongoing
💊 Medication Review
  • Cholinesterase inhibitors (donepezil, galantamine, rivastigmine) — Alzheimer's, DLB, PDD
  • Memantine — consider for moderate/severe Alzheimer's
  • Antipsychotics — justify continued use, document side effect discussion (CVD, diabetes, falls)
  • Anticholinergic burden — review all relevant medications (use ACB calculator)
⚖️ Legal, Welfare & End of Life
  • Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) — confirm or document if not in place
  • End of life preferences — record and ensure patient/carer know how to access palliative care
  • Consider adding to palliative care register
  • Named coordinator / key worker — record name and contact details
  • Confirm access to all appropriate benefits
🤝 Carer Assessment & Support
  • Identify carer(s) and record in EMIS
  • Confirm consent to communicate with carer — document
  • Assess impact of caring — note male carers less likely to volunteer distress
  • Offer carer health check (if at another practice, advise to contact own GP)
  • Offer carer psychoeducation — document if offered and accepted
  • Provide details of support services appropriate to illness stage
⚠️ Antipsychotic Prescribing Alert
  • Explicitly justify continued use at every review
  • Document plan to reduce or stop where possible
  • Record discussion of side effects with patient/carer — CVD risk, accelerated cognitive decline, falls
  • Do not continue without clear clinical rationale
📝 Coding & Documentation
  • Apply SNOMED dementia annual review code same day
  • Use (RP) LTC Review Tower Hamlets CEG v1.4 template in EMIS
  • Update or create care plan / advance care plan in record
  • Document all four domains addressed, actions and referrals made
  • Record LPA status and named coordinator
  • Record EOL preferences if discussed
