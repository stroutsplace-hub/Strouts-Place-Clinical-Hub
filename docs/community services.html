Tower Hamlets — Lymphoedema Service

Referral Criteria
✔ Inclusion Criteria
  • Chronic oedema / lymphoedema
  • History of repeated cellulitis related to oedema
  • Primary lymphoedema
  • Secondary lymphoedema to cancer
  • Systemic causes ruled out
  • Housebound patients: refer via Single Point of Access for first-line assessment before joint visit
✗ Exclusion Criteria
  • Known ischaemia of the affected limb
  • Varicose veins
  • Routine Doppler in absence of swelling
  • Venous or other ulceration
  • Unstable cardiac / renal failure
  • Absence of swelling
Source: Tower Hamlets Lymphoedema Referral — Accelerate CIC ↗

Tower Hamlets — Wound Care Service

Referral Criteria
✔ Inclusion Criteria
  • Complex wounds
  • Mobile leg ulceration
  • Pressure ulcer patients (grades 2–4) from Nursing Homes only
  • Housebound patients: refer via Single Point of Access for first-line assessment before joint visit
✗ Exclusion Criteria
  • Routine Doppler
  • Varicose veins
  • Housebound pressure ulcers (refer to Single Point of Access)
  • Chronic swelling / lymphoedema — refer via lymphoedema form
  • Foot ulceration / diabetic foot — to be seen by Foot Health
Source: Tower Hamlets Wound Care Referral — Accelerate CIC ↗
